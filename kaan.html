About Me

I am a Computer Engineering student at TED University, with a deep interest in 3D reconstruction and modeling. As the member of the team for 3D-EcoMap, my main focus is on converting 2D satellite images into detailed 3D models. This process allows us to accurately analyze road slopes and optimize route planning, which is central to providing fuel-efficient and eco-friendly driving routes. Through this project, I aim to contribute to advancing the field of 3D image processing and help develop innovative solutions for sustainable driving.
