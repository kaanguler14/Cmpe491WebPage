About Us

Meet the Team

We are a passionate group of Computer Engineering students at TED University, working on the 3D-EcoMap project to create a more efficient and sustainable world. Our team is dedicated to developing innovative solutions for real-time route optimization based on 3D satellite images and road slopes.

  • Alara Sermutlu - Developer
  • Kaan Güler - Developer
  • Rabia Yazıcı - Developer

Our Mission

Our mission is to reduce the environmental impact of transportation by providing drivers with eco-friendly, fuel-efficient routes based on real-time 3D modeling of roads and slopes. Through this project, we aim to combine advanced image processing and 3D visualization technologies to make everyday driving more sustainable.
